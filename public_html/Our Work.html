Loan and Funeral Policy Process

Loan Process:

To apply for a small loan, you need to provide the following documents:

  • 3 months bank statement
  • ID
  • Payslip

Once we receive your documents, we evaluate your affordability based on your credit profile and monthly expenses. If you qualify, you get approved for the loan.

Funeral Policy Process:

Applying for our funeral policy requires information about the applicant and dependants:

  • Applicant Information: Personal details of the person applying for the policy.
  • Dependants Information: Details of up to 8 dependants.

After providing the necessary information, we provide you with a quotation. Upon acceptance, you pay a monthly fee. In the event of a dependent's passing, you can request funeral cover.
